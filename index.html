    Hi!

    I’m Dong, a physical oceanographer, currently a PhD student at University of East Anglia. My research focuses on understanding the dynamics of ocean circulation and its interaction with climate variability. I use a combination of observational data analysis and numerical modeling to investigate these processes. Besides, I am interested in emerging areas, such as noval observations, deep-sea mining.
